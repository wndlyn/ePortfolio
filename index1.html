My Journey as a Student

In elementary school, Computer Science was never on my choice; I saw computers as tools for games but not a career path. In high school, while my classmates shared their plans, I felt uncertain about my future and ended up choosing Computer Science not out of passion but practicality, thinking, “Maybe I'll figure it out along the way.” The course proved challenging yet rewarding, teaching me problem-solving, critical thinking, and adaptability. I realized that the course you choose doesn't dictate your career forever; it's but a foundation. Life is rarely linear, and your future is shaped by learning, growth, and the passions you discover along the way.
